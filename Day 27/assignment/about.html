About Us

Chitwan Manav Sewa Ashram was established in 2072 B.S. (2015 A.D.) with a slogan "Human service is the best way of life". We are located at Bharatpur Chitwan. Chitwan Manav Sewa Ashram is taking care of oldage and differently-abled people. Every person is equal and have equal right. So, we started this organisation for taking care of needy ones. Not the order of the senses, but the call of the soul should be heard - everyone does it for themselves, but we should also be able to do good things for others. We provide fooding, housing and medical care for our loved ones. We are getting appreciations and people on different occasions like birthday, marriage anniversary, death anniversary and any other annual date or special date try to help our people by providing foods, clothes any many other goods. We'll continue our service and give importance to the needy ones. This gives us a true happiness.
